Our Legacy of Learning

Founded with a vision to provide world-class education in Chittagong, our school has been a beacon of knowledge for over two decades. We combine traditional values with modern pedagogical approaches to prepare our students for the challenges of the 21st century.

At Chittagong Divisional School, we believe that every child is unique. Our mission is to create a stimulating environment where students feel safe, supported, and inspired to reach their full potential.
